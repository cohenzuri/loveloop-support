Welcome to LoveLoop

LoveLoop helps couples track and celebrate their relationship activities together. Whether it's hugs, kisses, dates, or quality time, LoveLoop makes it easy to set goals and stay connected.

Getting Started

1. Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and password. Your data is securely stored and synced across all your devices.

2. Choose Your Mode

  • Solo Mode: Track your personal activities without pairing
  • Paired Mode: Connect with your partner to track shared activities together

3. Pair with Your Partner (Optional)

To pair with your partner:

  • One person generates a QR code or pairing code from the app
  • The other person scans the QR code or enters the pairing code
  • Start tracking your activities together!

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is securely stored using Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Your personal information and activity data are protected and never shared with third parties.

Can I use LoveLoop without an account?

Yes! You can use LoveLoop in local mode without creating an account. However, you won't be able to sync across devices or pair with a partner.

What happens if I unpair from my partner?

If you unpair, you'll keep your historical data, but you'll no longer see shared progress. You can enable solo mode to continue tracking activities on your own.

How do I change my activity goals?

Go to Settings > Goal Settings to customize your weekly targets for each activity type.

Does LoveLoop work on Apple Watch?

Yes! LoveLoop includes a companion Apple Watch app that syncs with your iPhone.

Troubleshooting

App is stuck on loading screen

This issue has been fixed in the latest version. If you still experience it:

  • Force quit the app and reopen it
  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're running the latest version

Pairing code not working

  • Make sure both users are signed in
  • Verify the pairing code is entered correctly
  • Check that both devices have internet connection
  • Try generating a new pairing code

Data not syncing

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're signed in to the same account on all devices
  • Force quit and reopen the app

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. LoveLoop collects only the data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information (email, name)
  • Activity data (tracked activities and goals)
  • Usage analytics (to improve the app)

We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your activity data is private between you and your partner (if paired).
